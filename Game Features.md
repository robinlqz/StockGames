**Game Features** 

✅ **4 players per group** with roles: AI, Financials, Real Estate, Baseline 

✅ **20 rounds** of trading 

✅ **Treatment messages** shown to each group except Baseline 

✅ **Portfolio allocation** between stocks (market returns) and bonds (4% per round) 

✅ **Valuation competition** - winner gets $10,000 per round 

✅ **Starting endowment** of $100,000 

✅ **Final payoff** = Portfolio value + total valuation bonuses
